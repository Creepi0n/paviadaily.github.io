Debris Crisis Continues On

Ekko Nuhmi

Tags: Ancient Debris

For another week in a row, the global debris crisis has also hit Pavia’s economy and consumers, despite high demand there are hardly any suppliers, why is that?

The geopolitical situation in recent months, above all the tense situation at the North Pole and on the north-west world border caused by the ‘wet bandits’, has paralysed the international economy. Various companies have turned to government contracts and neglected the public market to protect the local nations.

This development and the shortage of skilled labourers in the Nether have led to a bottleneck in the digging of new debris veins. Only sporadic offers at fence prices can be found on back markets.

However, the market seems to be slowly picking up again, and newcomer mining companies seem to be slowly meeting the demand.
